Pose of the Day

Today's pose is Tree Pose. Tree pose strengthens thighs, calves, ankles and spine, stretches inner thighs, chest and shoulders, improves sense of balance, relieves sciatica, and reduces flat feet.

Word of the Week

The word of the week is Karuna, sanskrit for compassion. Some time ago, a buddhist priest lived high in the mountains. He had a tiny bowl and fine spoon for food. One day he took a long walk in the woods. When he returned, he sees a robber running away with his bowl. The priest calls out in earnest, "Wait! You forgot the spoon!"
